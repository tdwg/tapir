Resource Directory (RDDL) for TAPIR

Overview

The TAPIR root namespace (http://rs.tdwg.org/tapir/) is currently being used for different TAPIR related stable resources. Specification related documents are available for each version under the respective RDDL subdirectories. Other general resources such as output models and query templates are listed her.
